Lake Sawa

Sawa Lake is located near the Euphrates river in Muthanna province in Iraq, about 23 km west of the city of Samawah. There is no flow of water in the lake except from the Euphrates River, where water flows to and from underground aquifers through cracks, Water levels vary during the seasons, and water does not dry because of the balance between evaporation and water access to the lake from the Euphrates.
